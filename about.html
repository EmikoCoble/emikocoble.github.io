About Me

This is what I do.

Hello! My name is Emiko Coble, my greatest passion is being able to help someone in need and to be a problem solver. I love to teach and share new ideas. I am a fantastic listener and I am good at finding answers to questions. I have a keen eye for things that are out of place and at my job I do minor corrections to MARC records in call number titles. If you’re looking for something and can’t find it, I will be on the hunt looking and searching to where it may be. I have extensive knowledge with the OCLC catalog and I am quick learner and my work shows it and I catch on pretty fast. I am a woman of few words and I’m brief and to the point. I love to investigate and question things that don’t seem fit in right.

My primary objective is to secure a full time position while also furthering my education by finishing my BA at (NEIU) Northeastern Illinois University and continue on by obtaining a MLIS degree at University of Wisconsin-Madison. I aspire to be a geographic planner and hope to make a career out of it.
